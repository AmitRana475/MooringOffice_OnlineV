2.1 NOTIFICATIONS

NOTIFICATION

The DigiMoor X7 software incorporates a state-of-the-art alerting systems with its Audio/Visual notifications.

 

These notifications are generated for following functions -

    1.      Inventory

a)      When count of minimum required ropes is below desired.

b)      When count of minimum required rope tails is below desired.

 

    2.      Inspection - Inspection due dates depends on the inspection criteria set by the Company, which may change time to time.

            For Lines, Rope Tail & Loose Equipment -

a)         7 days before inspection due date.

b)        Inspection due date.

c)         Inspection overdue.

 

    3.      Discard - Discard criteria depends on the inspection / discard criteria set by the Company, which may change time to time.

4.       

            For Lines & Rope Tail -

a)      Pre-Warning - When inspection rating reaches rating '6'.

b)      Discard warning - When inspection rating reaches rating '7'.

c)      Maximum running hours approaching (20% less than maximum running hours).

d)      Maximum running hours exceeded.

e)      Maximum allowable time approaching (12 months before maximum allowed time).

f)       Maximum allowable time exceeded.

 

            For Loose Equipment -

a)      Maximum allowable time approaching (12 months before maximum allowed time).

b)      Maximum allowable time exceeded.

 

    5.      End to End

For Lines / Rope -

a)      End to End Approaching (1 month before End to End due date)

b)      End to End Due

c)      End to End Overdue

 

      6.      Winch Rotation

            For Lines / Rope -

a)      Rotation Approaching (1 month before End to End due date)

b)      Rotation Due

c)      Rotation Overdue

 

    7.      Cropping

For Lines /  Rope -

a)      When rope is cropped above allowable 10% of its entire length.

 

    8.   Damaged

For Lines , Rope Tail & Loose Equipment -

a)      When any rope is spliced

 

     9.      Splicing

For  Lines / Rope -

a)      When any rope is spliced

 

    10.  Discarded

            For Lines, Rope Tail & Loose Equipment -

a)      When any rope is discarded

 

    11.  Disposed

           For Lines , Rope Tail & Loose Equipment -

a)      When any rope is disposed

 

Should any Deviation arise due to any reason, the DigiMoor X7 icon on the Taskbar will turn red in colour and it will automatically generate an alarm as well as the notification displaying there exists a Deviation which has not been acknowledged. DigiMoor X7 generates Audio & Visual Alarms. User has been provided with facility to acknowledge deviations. Facility has also been provided to acknowledge multiple of all Deviations simultaneously. To acknowledge multiple Deviation, select the Deviation that you want to acknowledge using checkbox, then click on Acknowledge button to Acknowledge all selected values. 

 

Moving pages - There are arrows displayed on top centre of the page to move the pages (previous / next symbols). Single arrow denotes single page shift, double arrow donates first and last page respectively.
