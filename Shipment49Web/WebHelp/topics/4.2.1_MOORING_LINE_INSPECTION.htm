4.2.1 MOORING LINE INSPECTION

MOORING LINE INSPECTION

                                   

      This option is available to list and create Mooring Lines inspection details. All past inspections are listed on this page.

      To view any past year data, click on the Year dropdown and press 'Search' button besides to list past year data. By default the current year dropdown is selected.

             New Inspection: Click on this button to add a new inspection of Mooring Lines. 

 

 

  • Mooring Line Inspection View -

            This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Lines > Mooring Lines Inspection > Add Inspection option.

 

 

The options on this page are listed below with respective details -

Inspection Date: Calendar to choose the actual date when the selected Lines were inspected.

Inspection By: Generally Name & Rank of the person inspecting to be inserted here.

Inspection Guidance: Click on this button to download Infographic guidance pdf on the ratings to be marked during an inspection, basis rope conditions.

Download Inspection Excel Sheet: Click this button to download an automatically generated excel sheet containing all available Lines onboard. This can be printed and carried along during inspection for ease of use.

Checkbox: Checkbox besides each rope should be clicked to enter the inspection data. Only Lines which are checked would be stored as inspected.

Zone A: Chock or Fairlead Contact area zone. The result of inspection of the rope which remains in this zone to be noted in the available fields below this section.

Zone B: Winch Drum Contact area zone. The result of inspection of the rope which remains in this zone to be noted in the available fields below this section.

External: Compulsory field, External rating of the rope basis its condition. Please follow the inspection guidance as above. The ratings are selectable from 1 to 7, 1 being the best condition and 7 worst condition which should be discarded.

Internal: Compulsory field, Internal rating of the rope basis its condition. Please follow the inspection guidance as above. The ratings are selectable from 1 to 7, 1 being the best condition and 7 worst condition which should be discarded.

Photo 1 & Photo 2: Non-Compulsory field. Facility has been provided to attach photographs (maximum 2) per rope. The maximum size of photo attachment is 200 kbs only.

Other fields are as mentioned in the labels.

Save: After inserting Inspection data, click on Save button to Save. If Save is not clicked, data will not be saved.

Cancel: Click on this button if the user does not want to save the inserted data.
