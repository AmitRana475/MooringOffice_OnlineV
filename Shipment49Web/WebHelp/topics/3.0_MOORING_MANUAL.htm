3.0 MOORING MANUAL
MOORING MANUAL

 

Mooring manual section incorporates all the 'Manual Text, images and attachments' part of the software and is displayed as 'e-manual'. These may contain MSMP (Mooring System Management Plan), LMP (Line Management Plan) and Training Content.

Each Manual may be divided into various sections and subsections.

To access these section, click on the desired menu name from the left sidebar submenu, and the relevant section will appear in the right side besides.

The manuals text are 'Read Only' and contains four sections -

a)      General Data

      This section is applicable to 'All Ships of the Company' and contains the e-manuals applicable to the MSMP (Mooring System Management Plan), LMP  (Line  Management Plan) and Training Content.

For easy scrolling, 'Previous' and 'Next' buttons are provided to the User, which will take the user to previous or next section / subsection as listed in the manual.

    

 b. Ship Specific Data

This section is 'Ship-specific' which is applicable to individual Ship only, and contains ship-specific sections from the manual.

        

 c)      Ship Specific Attachments

This section is available to attach the Ship specific documents. The list of minimum required attachments may be obtained from your Company's office.

The attachments may either be shared by your office or may be attached by the Ship staff themselves.

Attachments that are shared by the office will be received in a folder, which is to be directly imported via 'Import Text' and 'Import Attachment' from the 'Import' sub-menu under 'Administration' menu.

The attachments which are shared by the office appear in the list in relevant section only and is not possible to be deleted. To delete these, please request your office to delete the same from their Office-end version and reshare the 'Import Text' file, which should be imported in the Ship via  Import system.

Attachments can be only of following types -

Pdf, MS Word, MS Excel and Images.

 

 

 

d)     Revisions

This section is applicable to 'All Ships of the Company' and lists the various revision numbers of the sections and subsections.

The revision numbers are section specific only. The revision numbers are automatically generated via the office-end and gets updated with the latest 'Import Text' file.

 

Other functions of the Mooring Manual Section include -

a)      Search -

The Search option may be utilized to search any text from the complete manual.

To Search, type the desired text eg: LDBF and press the 'Search' button besides, this will open a page with the relevant results from each section where this text keyword was located. Now click on the desired section to view the excerpt of the text containing the Search term.

b)      PDF  -

The PDF download option is available for each page of the manual.

On pressing the PDF button, it will create and download the section which is currently open on the screen.

c)      Print -

To print text of a particular section, create & download its pdf via the above PDF download procedure. The option to print shall now appear within the PDF file.
