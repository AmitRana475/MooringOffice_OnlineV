2.4 ARCHIVES

ARCHIVES

The unacknowledged notifications are displayed on the default screen. To check any previous acknowledged notifications, click on 'Archives' button & select 'From' & 'To' Date from the calendar. To return to default page, press 'Go Back' button on the extreme right side of the page.
