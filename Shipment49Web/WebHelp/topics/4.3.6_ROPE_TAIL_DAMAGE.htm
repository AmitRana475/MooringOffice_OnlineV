4.3.6 ROPE TAIL DAMAGE

ROPE TAIL DAMAGE

          This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Rope Tail > Rope Tail Damage

 

 

Rope Tail Damage : Click on the 'Add Damage Rope Tail' button to make an entry of Rope Tail Damage operation, which shall open the form. Form fields are explained as below -

a)       Select Rope Tail: Dropdown to select the available rope tails as per their certificate numbers.

b)     Outboard End in Use (A/B): View only field

c)      Rope Tail winch assigned: View only field

d)     Rope Tail Location assigned: View only field

e)      Damage Observed: Dropdown to select the operation when damage was located i.e. during 'Inspection' or 'Mooring operation'.

f)       Damage Date: Calendar to select date of damage observed.

g)      Incident Report: Dropdown to select 'Yes' or 'No'. Select 'Yes' if the damage resulted in an incident and report of incident is recorded.
