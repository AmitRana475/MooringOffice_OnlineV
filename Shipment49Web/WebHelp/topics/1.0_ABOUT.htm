ABOUT DIGIMOOR X7

 

'DigiMoor X7, an intelligent, 'easy to install - operate software', for efficient recordkeeping management of mooring loose gear on board Ships and its monitoring & analysis ashore.

DigiMoor X7 complies to the requirements of "Mooring Equipment Guidelines 4 (MEG 4)".

The software includes MSMP and LMP e-manuals and helps Ships in recordkeeping of Mooring ropes, rope tails and loose equipment lifecycle data for -

           1.      Mooring operations - Records complete details of Berth, environmental conditions, running hours & leads of ropes etc for each Port.

2.      Inspections of Ropes, Rope Tails and loose equipment.

3.      Ropes, rope tails and loose equipment details.

4.      Lifecycle records of ropes, rope tails and loose equipment from receipt to disposal

 

In addition, it incorporates an approx. calculator for checking factor of safety for mooring ropes at each berth as per the load they are expected to experience at the input environmental conditions.
