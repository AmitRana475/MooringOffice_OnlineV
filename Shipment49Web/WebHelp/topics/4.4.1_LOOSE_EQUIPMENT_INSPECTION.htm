4.4.1 LOOSE EQUIPMENT INSPECTION

LOOSE EQUIPMENT INSPECTION

 

This option is available to list and create Mooring loose equipment inspection details. All past inspections are listed on this page.

To view any past year data, click on the Year dropdown and press 'Search' button besides to list past year data. By default the current year dropdown is selected.

 

 New Inspection: Click on this button to add a new inspection of Loose Equipment.  

Mooring Loose Equipment Inspection View -

This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Loose Equipment > Loose Equipment Inspection > Add LE Inspection option. ( As per the Required Equipment)

The options on this page are listed below with respective details -

Inspection By: Generally Name & Rank of the person inspecting to be inserted here.

Inspection Date: Calendar to choose the actual date when the selected loose equipment were inspected.

Loose Equipment Type: Dropdown list to select from the list of Loose equipment.

On choosing the loose equipment type, the page will display all available loose equipment onboard, following options are available to fill after that -

a)      Equipment Type: View only field (as selected equipment type).

b)     Number: View only field - Identification / Certificate number of the equipment as inserted when added in the Loose Equipment Details form.

c)      Condition: Dropdown to choose from 'Excellent' or 'Good' or 'Acceptable' or 'Not Acceptable'.

d)     Remarks: Text Box to insert remarks, if any.

e)      Photo 1 & 2: Non Compulsory Option to insert photographs for each loose equipment during inspection.

 

Download Inspection Excel Sheet: Click this button to download an automatically generated excel sheet containing all available loose equipment onboard. This can be printed and carried along during inspection for ease of use.

Save: After inserting Inspection data, click on Save button to Save. If Save is not clicked, data will not be saved.

Cancel: Click on this button if the user does not want to save the inserted data.
