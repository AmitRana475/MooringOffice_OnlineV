4.4.3 LOOSE EQUIPMENT DAMAGE

LOOSE EQUIPMENT DAMAGE

                 This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Loose Equipment > Loose Equipment Damage

Loose Equipment Damage :

  •       Select Equipment Type: S elect the Tab available loose equipment types.
  •     Click on the 'Add Loose Eq Damage' button to make an entry of Loose Equipment Damage operation.
  •        Certificate No. : Dropdown populates after selection of Loose equipment type. All Loose equipment associated with a particular type will be available here. 
  •        Damage Observed: Dropdown to select the operation when damage was located i.e. during 'Inspection' or 'Mooring operation'.
  •       Damage Date: Calendar to select date of damage observed.
  •       Reason Observed: Dropdown to select 'Yes' or 'No'. Select 'Yes' if the damage resulted in an incident and report of incident is recorded.
