2.3 ACKNOWLEDGE

NOTIFICATION - ACKNOWLEDGE

 

Each notification should be acknowledged before exporting data to the office.

      To Acknowledge Notification -

               a)   Click on the checkbox besides the notification.

                   b)   Press the 'Acknowledge' button on top left side.

                   c)    After the notification is acknowledged, it will turn to black colour and then move in Archives.

d)   You may select all notifications and acknowledge together. To do this, click on the topmost checkbox, which will automatically select all notifications on default page and then click the 'Acknowledge' button on top left side.
