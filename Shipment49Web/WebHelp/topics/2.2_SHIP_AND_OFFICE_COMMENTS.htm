2.2 SHIP AND OFFICE COMMENTS

NOTIFICATION - SHIP AND OFFICE COMMENTS

Ship Comments - Every notification has a facility to be commented upon. A simple link to 'Add Comment' is displayed besides each notification, which when clicked opens another window to comment.

To write comment, press 'Add Comment' button, write text in the text box and then press 'Save' button.

Multiple comments can be written for each notification.

All ship comments will be saved in the list.

All ship comments get exported to the office via the export file.

 

Office Comments - Every notification has a facility to view Office Comments. Office end comments are imported via the import file from the office.

All Unread Office Comments are highlighted on top of the page.

When clicked on the office comment link, it automatically turns 'read' from 'unread' mode.

Office comments are non-editable from Shipboard version.
