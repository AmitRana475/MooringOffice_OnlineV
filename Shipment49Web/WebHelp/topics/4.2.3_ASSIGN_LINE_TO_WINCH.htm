4.2.3 ASSIGN LINE TO WINCH

ASSIGN LINE TO WINCH

This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Line > Assign Line to Winch

 

 

 

 

The options on this page are listed below with respective details -

Active Records: List of Liness assigned to winch currently

Past Records: Past records of assignment and rotation on winches.

Assign Line to Winch: Click on this button to assign Line to winch, which shall open the form. Form fields are explained as below -

                        a)       Select Line: Dropdown to select the available lines as per their certificate numbers.

                        b)     Outboard End in Use (A/B): Select the current outboard end as per your Ship's marking. Line ends to be marked with A and B at opposite ends. After reeling on the winch, select the end currently on outboard side.

                        c)      Assigned Date: Selection of date when the line was reeled on the winch.

                        d)     Assign Line to Winch: Dropdown to select the desired Winch number where the selected line is currently reeled.

                        e)      Line location assign: View only field which appears after selection of the Winch number
