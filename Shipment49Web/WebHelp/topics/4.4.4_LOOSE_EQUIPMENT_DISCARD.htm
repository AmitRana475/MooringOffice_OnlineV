4.4.4 LOOSE EQUIPMENT DISCARD

LOOSE EQUIPMENT DISCARD

                         This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Loose Equipment > Loose Equipment Discard

Loose Equipment Discard :

  • Select Equipment Type: Select the TAB available for different loose equipment types.
  • Click on the 'Add Loose Equipment Discard' button to make an entry of Loose Equipment Discard operation, which shall open the form.
  • Certificate No. : Dropdown populates after selection of Loose equipment type. All Loose equipment associated with a particular type will be available here. 
  • Date Out of Service: Calendar to select date of Discard or Out of Service
  • Reason Out of Service: Dropdown to select reason for discard. 
  •  Damage Observed: Dropdown to select from options of 'Inspection' or 'Mooring Operation'. On selection of 'Mooring Operation', the system shows another dropdown below to select the name of the Operation.
