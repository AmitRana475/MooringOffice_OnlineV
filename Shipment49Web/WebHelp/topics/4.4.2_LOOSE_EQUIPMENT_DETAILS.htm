4.4.2 LOOSE EQUIPMENT DETAILS

LOOSE EQUIPMENT DETAILS

All Loose Equipment details shall be listed in this section.

             To add new Loose Equipment detail, click on 'Add Loose Equipment' button on top right side under the various type of Loose Equipment.(Shown in the RED Box) 

 

Loose Equipment Details Form View -

              This page appears from LMPR > Loose Equipment > Loose Equipment Details> Add Loose Equipment.

a)      Joining Shackle - Fields in the form are as below -

 

Identification number: Insert embedded no or Ship's own identification number assigned

Manufacturer Name: Text field

MBL (T): Maximum breaking load in tonnes

Type: Dropdown to select one value

Certificate Number: Number as per available certificate

Received Date: Calendar to insert date of receipt of that equipment

Installed Date: Calendar to insert date when equipment was put in service.

 

b)      Messenger Rope - Fields in the form are as below -

Manufacturer Name: Text field

Rope Construction: Text field to enter rope construction details.

Diameter (mm) : Enter rope diameter here in millimetres.

Length (mtrs) : Enter rope length here in metres.

MBL (T) : Enter rope MBL here in Tonnes.

LDBF (T) : Enter rope LDBF here in Tonnes.

WLL (T) : Enter rope WLL here in Tonnes.

Certificate Number: Enter the Rope Certificate Number here.

Received Date: Compulsory field, Calendar to select date of receipt of the rope. This date can only be a past date.

Installed Date: Non-compulsory field, Calendar to select the installation date. In case the rope is kept as non-used spare, please leave this field blank.

This field is connected to calculation of discard criteria, hence only apply when the rope is in actual use.

Rope Tagging: Dropdown to select Yes / No. Ropes are ge

Remarks: Text box to enter remarks related to the rope.

Save: Press Save button to save all data entered.
