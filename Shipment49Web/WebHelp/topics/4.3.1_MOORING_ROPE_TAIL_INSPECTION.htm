4.3.1 MOORING ROPE TAIL INSPECTION

MOORING ROPE TAIL INSPECTION

 

 

This option is available to list and create Mooring rope tail inspection details. All past inspections are listed on this page.

To view any past year data, click on the Year dropdown and press 'Search' button besides to list past year data. By default the current year dropdown is selected.

New Inspection: Click on this button to add a new inspection of Mooring Rope Tail.

-          Mooring Rope Tail Inspection View -

This page appears from LMPR > Mooring Rope > Mooring Rope Tail Inspection > Add New Inspection option.

 

 

The options on this page are listed below with respective details -

Inspection Date: Calendar to choose the actual date when the selected rope tails were inspected.

Inspection By: Generally Name & Rank of the person inspecting to be inserted here.

Inspection Guidance: Click on this button to download Infographic guidance pdf on the ratings to be marked during an inspection, basis rope conditions.

Download Inspection Excel Sheet: Click this button to download an automatically generated excel sheet containing all available rope tails onboard. This can be printed and carried along during inspection for ease of use.

Checkbox: Checkbox besides each rope tails should be clicked to enter the inspection data. Only rope tails which are checked would be stored as inspected.

External: Compulsory field, External rating of the rope tail basis its condition. Please follow the inspection guidance as above. The ratings are selectable from 1 to 7, 1 being the best condition and 7 worst condition which should be discarded.

Internal: Compulsory field, Internal rating of the rope tail basis its condition. Please follow the inspection guidance as above. The ratings are selectable from 1 to 7, 1 being the best condition and 7 worst condition which should be discarded.

Photo 1 & Photo 2: Non-Compulsory field. Facility has been provided to attach photographs (maximum 2) per rope tail. The maximum size of photo attachment is 200 kbs only.

Other fields are as mentioned in the labels.

Save: After inserting Inspection data, click on Save button to Save. If Save is not clicked, data will not be saved.

Cancel: Click on this button if the user does not want to save the inserted data.
